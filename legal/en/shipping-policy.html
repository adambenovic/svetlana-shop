Carrier and Delivery Methods

All orders are dispatched via Packeta (Zásilkovna). At checkout you can choose between:

  • Home delivery: the parcel is delivered to your address by the local courier partner. Estimated delivery: 3 – 7 business days after dispatch.
  • Pickup point (Z-Box / Zásielkovňa point): collect from any Packeta pickup location of your choice. Estimated delivery: 2 – 5 business days after dispatch.

Total estimated time from order to delivery is typically 5 – 12 business days. These estimates are not guaranteed delivery times.

VAT and Customs

All listed prices include VAT. For deliveries within the European Union, the VAT rate of the destination country applies, and we account for that VAT through the EU One-Stop-Shop (OSS) scheme; this is the rate reflected on your invoice. As all destination countries are within the European Union, there are no import duties, customs fees, or additional taxes for the recipient.

Electrical Waste (WEEE) and Packaging

Our lamps include an LED light source and therefore qualify as electrical and electronic equipment (EEE) under Directive 2012/19/EU on waste electrical and electronic equipment (WEEE). The crossed-out wheeled bin symbol marked on the product or its documentation means that the product must not be discarded with unsorted municipal waste at the end of its life. Instead, it must be handed in at a designated collection point for waste electrical and electronic equipment, so that it can be recovered and recycled. Correct disposal helps protect the environment and human health.

In accordance with our producer take-back obligations, you may return end-of-life equipment of an equivalent type to us; please contact us at contact@svetlanalampe.sk to arrange this.

Product packaging is recyclable. Please sort packaging materials (cardboard, plastics) according to the waste-separation rules in your country.
