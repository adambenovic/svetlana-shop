About Cookies on this Website

This website uses only a small number of cookies that are necessary to make the site work and to remember your basic preferences. We do not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking cookies, and we do not profile you or share cookie data with advertising networks. This page explains exactly which cookies we set and how you can control them.

For full information on how we process personal data, see our Privacy Policy.

Cookies We Use

We set only the following essential and functional cookies:

  • cart — essential — remembers the items in your shopping cart.
  • currency — functional — remembers the currency you selected (EUR, CZK, PLN or HUF).
  • language — functional — remembers the language you selected.
  • cookie-consent — essential — stores whether you accepted or declined cookies.
  • cookie-banner-dismissed — essential — remembers that you have dismissed the cookie banner so it is not shown again.

These cookies are first-party (set by this website only), contain no advertising identifiers, and are not used to track you across other websites.

Consent and the Cookie Banner

Because we use only cookies that are strictly necessary or that provide functionality you have requested (such as remembering your currency and language), these cookies are exempt from the consent requirement under the ePrivacy Directive 2002/58/EC. Our cookie banner therefore offers a simple Accept / Decline choice and records that choice; no non-essential cookies are set in either case, because we do not use any.

If we introduce analytics or marketing cookies in the future, we will ask for your prior consent through the banner before any such cookie is set, and we will update this page accordingly.

Managing Your Preferences

You can manage cookies at any time:

  • Re-open the cookie banner: click the "Cookie preferences" link in the footer of any page.
  • Browser controls: all major browsers let you delete cookies that have already been set or refuse new cookies. Refer to your browser's help pages for instructions. Blocking essential cookies may prevent the shopping cart from working.

Third-Party Recipients

We do not set any third-party advertising or analytics cookies. Our payment provider (GoPay) and our delivery provider (Packeta / Zásielkovňa) may set their own cookies only on their own pages when you are redirected there during payment or parcel tracking; those cookies are governed by their respective privacy notices.
